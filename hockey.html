My Passion: Hockey

I began watching hockey in 2017 when Vegas first got a team. The Vegas Golden Knights were an expansion team, but made it to the Stanley Cup in year 1. From then on, I have thoroughly enjoyed going to hockey games as it has quickly become my favorite sport to watch. This past year, in 2023, the Vegas Golden Knights won the Stanley Cup for the first time and I was lucky enough to be there in person to witness it. I consider it to be one of the best days of my life.

Reasons I Love the Vegas Golden Knights

  1. Fan Experience
    • Best arena in the NHL for entertainment and intensity
    • Loyal fanbase
  2. High-Level Gameplay
    • Exciting players to watch
    • Top-tier coaching
  3. 2023 Stanley Cup Victory
    • Winning Game: June 13, 2023
    • Unforgettable celebrations
